The Challenge

Service businesses lose revenue when customers are geographically scattered:

  • Excessive drive time and wasted fuel
  • Fewer appointments completed per day
  • Higher operational costs and crew fatigue
  • Isolated customers that aren't profitable to serve

How GDS Can Help

We combine geospatial intelligence with enriched local address data to turn your customer data into actionable insights:

  • Map your current customer base
  • Identify isolated, low-margin service areas
  • Locate high-potential neighborhoods for expansion
  • Target new prospects within 0.1–10 miles of existing customers
  • Visualize customer value by spend level
  • Increase cross-sell opportunities with smart clustering
